Programs & Services

Free Haircut

Experience the transformative power of community and care with our free haircut service, pioneered by Chairman Josefino Manicad. At the heart of our initiative lies a commitment to fostering connections and uplifting spirits within our community. Whether you're looking for a fresh new style or simply seeking a moment of relaxation, our skilled stylists are here to provide top-notch service at absolutely no cost to you. Step into our welcoming space and leave feeling rejuvenated, not just by your new look, but by the sense of belonging and solidarity that our community fosters.

Clean and Green

Join us in the mission for a cleaner, greener future with our Clean and Green Program spearheaded by Chairman Josefino Manicad. Together, we are transforming our community into a thriving oasis of sustainability and environmental stewardship. Through innovative initiatives and hands-on activities, we're making strides in reducing waste, conserving resources, and nurturing green spaces. Whether it's planting trees, organizing clean-up drives, or promoting eco-friendly practices, every action counts towards building a healthier planet for generations to come.

Health

Discover a path to wellness and vitality with our Health Program led by Chairman Josefino Manicad. We're dedicated to empowering individuals and communities to prioritize their health and well-being through education, access to healthcare services, and proactive lifestyle choices. From free health screenings to fitness classes and nutritional workshops, we offer a holistic approach to improving overall health. Our goal is to foster a culture of wellness where everyone has the resources and support they need to thrive. Join us on this journey to a healthier future, where each step forward is a step towards a happier, more vibrant life.

Bible Study

Come join us for our Bible Study program! We get together to read and talk about the Bible. It's a friendly group where everyone is welcome, no matter what you believe. We think it's important to understand what the Bible says because it can help us live better lives and understand more about ourselves and our world. You can ask questions if you want, and we'll try to answer them together. It's a great way to learn and grow in our faith and understanding. So, if you're curious about the Bible or just want to spend some time with friendly people, come and join us.

Project Bayanihan

Let's come together and make a difference with our Bayanihan Project! Inspired by the Filipino tradition of bayanihan, where communities unite to achieve a common goal, our project aims to address important needs and challenges in our community. Whether it's building homes for those in need, cleaning up public spaces, or providing assistance to families affected by disasters, we believe that by working together, we can create positive change and uplift those around us. Everyone has a role to play, and every contribution, no matter how small, makes a big impact.

Feeding Program

Come and be part of our Feeding Program! We gather to share food with those who might not have enough to eat. It's a chance for us to come together as a community and help each other out. We believe that everyone deserves to have a full stomach and a warm meal. Whether you're in need of food or you want to lend a hand in serving, there's a place for you here. Our program is a way to show compassion and support to those facing hunger. Together, we can make a difference by ensuring that no one goes to bed hungry. So come join us and let's spread kindness one meal at a time.

The Barangay II-C, City of San Pablo

This marked the creation of Barangay II-C also known as "Uson" Simprosio C. Ramos was the first head of this barangay. The Barangay Hall in Major Eseo Street was conducted during the term of Chairman Ramos who served his constituents until a synchronized election was held in 1989 won by Ireneo Perez.
