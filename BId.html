Barangay I.D

Guide: How To Get Barangay ID
There are several steps you must take to get a Barangay ID, which we will discuss in this section.
Barangay ID Requirements 2024
Prepare the following things before you get a Barangay ID.

  • Proof that you are a Filipino citizen; birth certificate
  • Proof of residency via census; you can use a document like a billing statement
  • 1×1 or 2×2 picture; shot for ID purpose (formal picture)
  • Certificate of employment from the last employer (if you work in this barangay)
You must have been a resident of the barangay for at least six months. This requirement, however, is not always followed.


Procedures On How to Get a Barangay ID
Here are the steps in how to get a Barangay ID:
  • Go to your Barangay Hall
  • Look for the Barangay Captain or any assigned councilor for the day
  • Fill out the application form (1)
  • Submit your documents
  • Pay the fee if there is any
  • Wait for further instructions how to claim the ID


How to get Barangay ID Online
You cannot get a Barangay ID through any online means. Barangays are small geographical places that have no budget for an online system like that. There is no system for barangay id online registration. The national government also has no centralized system in place that barangays can use for ID issuance.

The Barangay II-C, City of San Pablo

This marked the creation of Barangay II-C also known as "Uson" Simprosio C. Ramos was the first head of this barangay. The Barangay Hall in Major Eseo Street was conducted during the term of Chairman Ramos who served his constituents until a synchronized election was held in 1989 won by Ireneo Perez.
